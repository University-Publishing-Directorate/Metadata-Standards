# 📚 University of M'sila - Metadata Standards Repository

Welcome to the official repository for academic journal metadata management at **Mohamed Boudiaf University – M’sila, Algeria**. This project is dedicated to ensuring high-quality indexing and global visibility for our research journals.

## 📖 About our Journals

### Journal of Social Sciences and Humanities (JOSSH)
The **Journal of Social Sciences and Humanities** is an international, open-access, double-blind peer-reviewed journal published bi-annually. Established in 2011, it covers diverse fields such as sociology, psychology, history, and related disciplines.
*   **Languages:** Arabic, English, and French.
*   **License:** Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial (CC BY-NC 4.0).
*   **ISSN:** 2253-010X | **E-ISSN:** 2602-6961.
